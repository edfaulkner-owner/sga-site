About SGA

The Standard in Sticker Authentication

Our Mission

The Sticker Grading Authority was founded to bring structure, consistency, and legitimacy to the world of collectible stickers and adhesive media. What began as a passion project has grown into the definitive standard for sticker authentication.

What We Grade

From vintage 1970s machine vending sheets to modern holographic limited releases, SGA evaluates sticker sets, trading card packs, wild specimens, and rare one-of-a-kind adhesive artifacts.

The Machine Registry

Our nationwide initiative to document, photograph, and track every sticker vending machine in the United States. Contributors use the Submit portal to add new machines — each marked by a spider on our live map.

Why Spiders?

Spiders build webs — intricate, patient networks that catch what others miss. The SGA spider represents our commitment to weaving a comprehensive map of sticker culture across America.
